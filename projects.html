Our Projects

1. BEACON AT NOOR

Team Bridging The Gaps is going to visit The Noor Grammar School and interact with the students, providing awareness on importance of higher education and skill learning in future.

We will be interacting with the students and also will have an interview session with them to know their views on education and skill learning and what they want to become in future and how they can achieve their goals.

An interview with the principal of the school will also be conducted to know the views of the school management.

Thanks for your patience.
